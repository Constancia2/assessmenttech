HYGIENE VOLUNTEERS

Our hygiene volunteer promoters are people who against all odds, have continued to create awareness about the importance of good hygiene and, sanitation in the rural areas, especially to our young girls and mothers.

WATER SUSTAINABILITY

We ensure adequate water sustainability in the rural community. We partner with other international bodice and government to ensure borehole and tap water in rural areas.

ERADICATION OF OPEN DEFECATION

We Strengthen government efforts to eradicate the practice of open defecation. We help provide toilet in various communities , thereby educating them on the importance of good sanitation and proper hand wash after visiting the toilet.

SCHOOL SANITATION AND HYGIENE EDUCATION

This is to promote children's right to health and clean environment to influence a generational change in health promotion behaviour and attitudes.
