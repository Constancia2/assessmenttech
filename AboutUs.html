OUR MISSION, VISION AND VALUE

Drink Right is a charitable, nonprofit, humanitarian organization dedicated to the development and delivery of clean, safe water and sanitation solutions in developing communities and rural area. This organization exists to promote the development of high-quality drinking water and sanitation services, accessible to all, and sustained by strong communities, businesses, and governments

WATER CHANGES EVERYTHING

Safe water doesn’t just mean people gain hours back for themselves and their families. It means people live longer, healthier lives. Parents are more likely to see their children grow up. The economy expands. Political unrest decreases. Families can keep their kids in school. There’s more time for work, rest, and play. That’s why we’re doing everything we can to build a global movement to bring water to Everyone Forever.
